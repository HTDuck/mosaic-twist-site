Gameplay

Simple moves, satisfying matches.

Every puzzle starts with a board of square tiles. Move, rotate, and refine them until neighboring edges line up and the mosaic resolves.

1

Move

Move tiles from the bank onto the board, then swap and refine.

2

Twist

Rotate each tile in 90-degree steps.

3

Solve

Finish the board when every neighboring edge matches.
