Mosaic Twist Privacy Policy

Effective July 18, 2026. This policy explains how Mosaic Twist handles information in the app and on this website.

Information We Collect

Mosaic Twist does not require an account. Puzzle progress, settings, and best times are stored locally on your device. This website is static and does not use contact forms or analytics.

Local App Data

Puzzle progress, settings, and best times are stored locally on your device. Deleting the app may delete locally stored progress unless your device backup restores it.

Third-Party Services

When Mosaic Twist is downloaded through Apple's App Store, Apple's privacy practices may apply to App Store downloads and related Apple services. This website is hosted by GitHub Pages, which may process basic server logs to operate the service.

Changes

Features, content, and availability may change over time. We may update this policy when Mosaic Twist changes affect how information is handled. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@mosaictwist.com.
