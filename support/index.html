Mosaic Twist Support

Quick troubleshooting steps and FAQs for Mosaic Twist.

Quick Checks

  • Update Mosaic Twist to the latest available version.
  • Restart the app if a puzzle or saved game does not restore as expected.
  • Make sure your device has enough free storage for local progress.

Common Questions

Does Mosaic Twist require an account?

No. Puzzle progress is stored locally on your device.

Does Mosaic Twist need an internet connection?

No internet connection is required for normal puzzle play.

Where are the privacy policy and terms?

You can find them from the site navigation or footer.
