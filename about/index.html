About Mosaic Twist

Mosaic Twist is a relaxing puzzle game about moving and twisting square tiles until every neighboring edge fits into a finished mosaic.

How It Plays

Each puzzle starts with a board and a bank of square tiles. Move tiles around the board, twist them in 90-degree steps, and keep refining until the edges line up.

Mosaic Twist is designed for short, relaxing sessions with clear touch controls, local progress, best times, and handcrafted puzzle sets.

Gameplay Tips

  • Tap a tile to select it, then tap a destination to move it or swap it with another tile.
  • Drag tiles from the bank onto the board when you want a more direct move.
  • Tap a placed tile once to select it, then tap it again to rotate it in 90-degree steps.
  • Some puzzles start with locked tiles. Use them as anchors while you work out the rest of the board.
  • On larger puzzles, scroll the bank to see every available tile.
